About Me

Hello,

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Google DeepMind, and a final-year DPhil student in Computer Science at the University of Oxford, working with Phil Blunsom. I work on machine learning and natural language processing.

My work has been awarded a Best Long Paper award at ACL 2018, an Outstanding Long Paper award at EACL 2017, and a Nomination for Best Long Paper at ACL 2019. My DPhil study at Oxford is generously supported by an EPSRC Studentship under the Flexibility to Support the Very Best Students scheme, and a Balliol Mark Sadler scholarship.

Prior to this, I received a Research Master’s Degree in Language Technologies from the School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University, where I worked with Chris Dyer. I also completed an MSc in Computer Science at the University of Oxford, and a BEng in Informatics Engineering at Institut Teknologi Bandung.
